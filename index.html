Day of Digital Humanities 2017

Day of Digital Humanities 2017 - Gettysburg College was created as part of the international Day of Digital Humanities 2017. Digital humanities practictioners at Gettysburg College were asked to provide their thoughts on the digital humanities and how it impacts their teaching, learning, and research.

Technical information: This site is adapted from code originally by Jason Heppler and is available on GitHub. It works by pulling quotes from a .csv file using the D3.js data visualization JavaScript library.
