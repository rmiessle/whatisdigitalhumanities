Day of Digital Humanities 2017

This site was created as part of the international Day of Digital Humanities 2017. Digital humanities practictioners at Gettysburg College were asked to provide their thoughts on the digital humanities and how it impacts their teaching and research. Refresh the page to get a new quote, or just wait 60 seconds and it will change.

This site is adapted from code originally by Jason Heppler and is available on GitHub. Quotes are pulled from a .csv file using the D3.js JavaScript library.
